The Curriculum

Oak Groove University: Fostering Diversity and Service

Oak Groove University aims to cultivate a vibrant community where students from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds, academic strengths, and cultural heritages come together. Alongside academic achievement, the school champions preparing students to become active, contributing members of society. Here's how potential students can join this journey:

Admission

  1. Submit a completed application.
  2. Obtain a teacher's recommendation on your academic potential and character.
  3. Share your entrance exam scores and middle school transcripts.

Based on your application, you may receive either:

  1. Unconditional admission: Welcome aboard!
  2. Probationary Admission: Meet additional requirements before starting ninth grade.

For Transferees:

  1. Show us your high school transcript.
  2. Provide a letter from a counselor or teacher highlighting your academic potential and character.
  3. Chat with the Executive Director for Enrollment and/or Principal.
  4. If you're from abroad, demonstrate English proficiency.
  5. We generally welcome new students at the start of a quarter/semester, except in special cases.
