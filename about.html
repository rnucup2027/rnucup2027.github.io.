Our History

Humble Beginnings

In 2012, a group of aspiring educators, spearheaded by the esteemed Dr. Ah Yo Kona and Dr. Gus Tokom Atulog, envisioned a space where knowledge could flourish and young minds could be nurtured. With unwavering determination as fresh graduates, they established Oak Groove University, a small, but ambitious institution offering STEM education.

Period of Progression

Oak Groove Academy experienced a period of significant growth. The addition of new buildings and faculty members allowed the institution to expand its academic offerings and enroll a larger student body. This transformation marked a new era of academic excellence and solidified the institution's place as a leading educational center in the region.

Beacon of Innovation

The institution established its graduate school in 1965, offering master's and doctoral programs in various disciplines. Renowned scholars and researchers were drawn to the campus, further enhancing the academic reputation of Oak Grove. Today, OGU stands as a vibrant hub of academic inquiry, boasting a diverse student body, a distinguished faculty, and a commitment to cutting-edge research across a wide spectrum of fields.

Leaving a Legacy

Throughout its rich history, Oak Groove University has remained true to its core values of scholarship, integrity, and community. Notable alumni have gone on to achieve success in various fields, contributing significantly to society. From renowned scientists and business leaders to celebrated artists and writers, the OGU community has left an indelible mark on the world.

Future ft. OGU

As Oak Groove University embarks on its next chapter, it remains steadfast in its commitment to providing a transformative educational experience that fosters intellectual growth, personal development, and responsible leadership. The institution continues to invest in its faculty, facilities, and resources, ensuring that students have access to the tools and opportunities they need to thrive in a rapidly changing world. With its unwavering dedication to academic excellence and its commitment to empowering tomorrow's leaders, Oak Groove University stands poised to continue its legacy of shaping minds and enriching lives for generations to come.

Our Admin

Principal

The principal oversees the entire school, ensuring a safe and productive learning environment. They manage staff, curriculum, budget, and student well-being, acting as the school's public face.

Assistant Principal

The principal's right-hand, the assistant principal supports day-to-day operations. They handle discipline, scheduling, student concerns, and specific areas like academics or athletics, depending on the school.
