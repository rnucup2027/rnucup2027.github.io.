Swift's Songs as Sonnets

Taylor Swift + Sonnet + Us = YAY

Theme: Love has unpredictable and complex outcomes.

The Aftermath of Daylight

The poems explore the themes of self-discovery, heartbreak, and love that are commonly observed among the universal themes that Taylor Swift frequently explores in her songs. Her lyrics frequently elicit deep thought and insight from listeners and give them a fresh outlook on their own lives and experiences. We chose two different yet related albums from her life's work to show the parallelism of the outcomes of love and what it can do to us. In summary, these sonnets are the short overviews of intricate relationships.

CERALDE, Frediliz Rosette B.

Dress

Petrarchan

More than you want to

Our secret glances we share eye-wide,
They have no idea about you and I.
But, baby, it's getting hard to lie.
When you put your mark on me, I swollowed my pride.
This growing thought of being your bride can't seem to slide.
Whenever you're near me, it feels like a never-ending euphoric high.
This bewildering feeling that I go to bed with and cry,
Best friends? They don't wake up bedside and later hide.

No one knows me like you do.
You are the only one who matters to me,
Tell me, then, who do I run to?
So give me a memory that I can look back on with glee.
On a fantasy of just me and you,
Where I can love you with a guarantee.

The Man

Shakespearean

Happily ever After

Do all girls need a prince?
It doesn't mean I'm lonely when I choose to stand solitary,
Men have come and gone ever since.
That's my choice, and I'm not hurting anyone with my type of dictionary.

You should act ladylike and obey.
Everyone judging me; do you expect me to crumble down?
When the players play? It's just okay.
But it's time for my good ideas to be heard and to get my crown.

I'm so sick of running as fast as I can from men.
This time I'm only looking at me, myself, and I; that's the plan.
They get mad and bad; can't they comprehend?
Because if I were the man, I'd be the man.

To all of the girls fighting alone,
Honey, don't stop dancing till you get your throne.

NUCUP, Roann Alexa G.

Call It What You Want

Petrarchan

Joint Hearts Over Hatred Whispers

With faults and flaws exposed, my name ashamed,
Unready, I face the judgment's fierce gaze.
Unfazed by hate, my inner peace ablaze,
Amidst the dark storm, our love remained.
Protecting me from the sharp world unmaimed,
My lover bears all of the drama craze.
My heart finds solace in my lover's haze.
May others judge, our bond remains unflamed.

Old wounds left my heart guarded and secure.
False accusations cloud our bond, they say.
In your arms, past wounds and whispers endure.
Finding your love, an act both true and pure.
Amidst life's maze, you are my guiding ray.
With you, my wounds discovered its true cure.

I Forgot That You Existed

Shakespearean

Indifference Takes Over

In echoes' depths, how long do my aches dwell?
Your love's dark echoes haunt my troubled mind.
Neath your cloud, I was living in unwell.
With thoughts of grief and woe, my heart's depths twine.

Your name, a burden on my tongue-tied lips,
Your presence, haunting every waking hour,
A fixed stay in my mind's repeating clips,
In my thoughts, your deeds are in full contour.

But then one night, a cryptic, fine release,
A mantle of void gently did appear,
Your presence dwindled, lost in gentle peace.
I feared your absence, but my end is not near.

My tangled heart, too fraught to keep you close,
My fervor fades, detachment makes a pose.

SULA, Angeline Norin G.

Getaway Car

Petrarchan

An Unwanted, but Incomplete Close

Such conclusion should've been the one that I expected
Those short-lived moments bittersweet
I endowed us a quick fire yet you remained in your seat
I too turned a blind eye because I was yet to be affected
Seeking an excuse as I was uncontented
The road being broken in the previous street
I was beat but I didn't want to retreat
It was an ambush where the glass was tinted

The key was never about how fast it was
Keeping your eyes close to an ending that we chose
From the start we should have known it wasn't going to your accord
Stoked was I and the underlying danger was the cause
Now that the sweet moments came to a close
Staying is just something that I could not afford

Afterglow

Shakespearean

A Burnt Wish

To a shining jewel I delivered a blow
My baseless accusation caused you
I left impuissant who I thought was a foe
With the way you fell I can't help but wish I saw it through

I chose to drown myself and isolate
Made you suffer with my refusal to liaise
I cried too much and it gathered in a plate
And I wondered why I had to set you ablaze

I admit I'm at fault
I unknowingly added fuel to our spark
My intention was none of that sort
But I'm hoping that I left some kind of mark

So we can relive the smoke of dead embers
I'll enkindle it so don't surrender
